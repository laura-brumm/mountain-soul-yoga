Teacher Trainings

To further our mission to elevate the yoga community of Vail, Mountain Sould Yoga is committed to offering quality teacher education opportunities. As a mountain affiliate of the Colorado School of Yoga, we currently offer a 200-hour Vinyasa Yoga teacher training in addition to Restorative Yoga and Yin Yoga teacher trainings, and continuing education opportunities for existing teachers to hone their current skill set. Our trainings ensure that mountain yogis have access to the highest caliber of trainings available, while visitors can enjoy their education in a beautiful mountain setting.
