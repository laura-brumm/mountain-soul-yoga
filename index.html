Mountain Soul Yoga is committed to elevating the yoga community of Vail through exceptional yoga instruction and quality teacher education in an inspiring setting. Whether you are dropping into our dynamic yoga classes or joining us for an advanced teacher training, you will be enriched with practical knowledge, inspired by yogic philosophy and challenged to recognize what you are passionate about and pursue it wholeheartedly. Mountain Soul Yoga cultivates the unique aspects of mountain life that you are already drawn to: strength, courage, curiosity, and discernment.
