Ayurveda

Julia Clarke is a Certified Maharishi Ayurveda Wellness Consultant. She has studied this ancient Indian science of health and healing at the Maharishi University of Management in Fairfield, Iowa, and at AVN Argoya Ayurvedic Hospital in Madurai, India.

Discover the refreshing simplicity of cultivating your perfect health through Ayurveda, and reawaken your body's natural power to heal and experience true vitality. As the world's most ancient living tradition of self-care, Ayurveda is an incredible, systematic body of knowledge that spans thousands of years, and in today's world is more relevant and vital than ever.

In a demystifying consulation with Julia, discover how this Indian tradition recognizes you in a modern, relevant context. Learn to identify your digestion as the root of all illness and improve nutrient absorption and boost your immune system through diet, and receive routine and exercise recommendations that will radically shift your health and perspective. In an Ayurvedic Wellness Consultation, you will be given the tools to live fully in natural rhythm, season to season, by integrating daily self-care routines to promote immunity and radiance, as well as basic Ayurvedic cleansing principles to prevent buildup of toxins. Leave with an empowered sense of your own capactiy to self-heal, and recapture the balance you long for!
