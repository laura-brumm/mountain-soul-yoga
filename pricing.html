Join

At Mountain Soul Yoga we understand that everyone has a unique schedule and needs, so we offer different memebership packages to accommodate everyone from the daily practitioner to the seasonal visitor. Ask us about our private yoga sessions and check out our Teacher Trainings.

Annual Membership

For the Yoga Lover

Daily yoga practitioner? Commit to a full year of yoga with us and enjoy a reduced member rate with a rewarding member experience through all four seasons. Pay in full at time of purchase. Purchases are non-refundable.

Monthly Unlimited Autopay Membership

Great for Locals! 3 Month Minimum.

Ready to commit to your practice? Monthly Unlimited Autopay offers you the best value as a regular practitioner. Enhance your mountain lifestyle with unlimited yoga at a rate of $99 per month. Your unlimited access begins the moment you sign up and payments will be withdrawn each month thereafter. Autopay only. (3 month minimum. 30 days notice required for cancellation. No suspensions. No refunds.)

Month By Month

Ideal for Visitors

Perfect for seasonal visitors to the valley, this single month membership gets you full access to our public classes for 30 days so you can stay vital and strong while you're here in the mountains.

Ten Punch Card (Good for 6 Months)

For the Occasional Grazer

Looking for the occasional yoga class with no strings attached? Try a punch card and enjoy ten classes at a competitive rate, anytime. Punch cards can be shared so bring a friend! Punch cards expire after 6 months and are non-transferable and non-refundable.

Drop-In

Stop By, Give Us a Try

First time here? Looking for a last minute pick me up? Just visiting the valley? The drop in is the impulse buy that keeps on giving.

Group Membership

Strenght in Numbers!

Whether you are a group of friends, coworkers, or acquaintances, studies suggest that exercising together improves results, increases motivation, and strenghtens relationships. For groups of 6 or more, we offer our Unlimited Monthly Autopay membership at a 25% discount. Three month minimum. Email info@mountainsoulyoga.com for details.
